Christmas market in Aalborg

Here’s everything the Christmas heart desires…

The Christmas Market in Aalborg oozes and smells like Christmas. Here you can find a multitude of Christmas treats: mulled wine, burnt almonds and warm fritters – all of this and more in the small cozy Christmas cabins.

A bird’s-eye view of Aalborg

Don’t miss the opportunity to take a trip up the big Ferris wheel, where you can look out over the entire Christmas market and a large part of the city. Admire the city streets dressed in the most beautiful Christmas lights with spruce branches and big red Christmas hearts.

Meet Santa’s little helpers!

Hilmer Halmkvist and Von Grangren, the city's entertaining elves, can be found around the streets every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday from November 26.
